# ROI (RETURN ON INVESTMENT) MODEL - TEMPLATE
**Project Name:** AI-Powered Customer Engagement
**Goal:** Prove the economic value of the Conversational AI platform.

---

## 1. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)
| KPI | Pre-AI (Current) | Post-AI (Target) | Improvement |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **AHT (Avg. Handle Time)** | 8.5 min | 5.5 min | **35% Reduction** |
| **FCR (First Contact Resolution)** | 60% | 85% | **25% Improvement** |
| **IVR Containment** | 20% | 55% | **35% Increase** |
| **Cost per Interaction** | $4.50 | $1.20 | **73% Reduction** |

## 2. PROJECTED SAVINGS (Annual)
### 2.1 Agent Productivity
- **Volume:** 1,000,000 interactions / year.
- **Reduction in AHT:** 3 mins saved per call.
- **Total Hours Saved:** 50,000 agent hours / year.
- **Cost Savings:** (Assuming $20/hour cost) = **$1,000,000 saved / year**.

### 2.2 Operational Efficiency
- **Fewer Transfers:** 150,000 calls / year resolved by AI that would have needed a transfer.
- **Estimated Savings:** $2.50 per transfer = **$375,000 saved / year**.

## 3. ESTIMATED ROI (3-YEAR MODEL)
- **Year 1:** Implementation Cost ($250k) - ROI: -15%
- **Year 2:** Full Operational Value - ROI: **240%**
- **Year 3:** Scaled Solution - ROI: **480%**

---

## 4. STRATEGIC BENEFITS (Intangible)
- **Employee Satisfaction:** Agents focus on complex, high-value tasks, reducing turnover.
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT/NPS):** Faster resolution and 24/7 availability improve brand loyalty.
- **Data Insights:** AI captures 100% of customer sentiment and intent data for business intelligence.
